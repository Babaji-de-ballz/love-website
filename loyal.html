Loyal

By Miggy Mayuga

Loyalty isn’t just a word I say,
It’s shown in the choices I make each day.
Not when it’s easy, not when it’s bright,
But in the dark, in the hardest fight.

I stand my ground when others leave,
Hold to truth when few believe.
Not bound by gain, nor swayed by fear,
But prove my love when storms are near.

So judge not words, but what I do,
For loyalty stays when friends are few.
It lives within my heart so true,
Through every storm, I’ll see it through.
