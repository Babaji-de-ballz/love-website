Lesson

By Jay Aldrich Catibog

Love can teach a good lesson
Or it can changed you
and turn to a bad person
Other else it can make u a silent person

Thinking of you and wait for a sign
Is just walking on the way with no sign
sometimes i think that im bad at love
Waiting lightning to struck at my trunk

Loving you in a quiet moment
Is just staring on the moon with no comment
And my mind and eyes search for you
Knowing youre in the same place too

And in the end i confess my love on you
Knowing you dont have to like me too
And i say i like you in front of you
And you replied with respect say i dont like you

I dont know if i hate myself or hate you
Because you refuse the love that i save on you
It all i wanted is to be with you
To prove my love and make it true

In the end i learn a lesson from you
I know that i cant find a girl like you
And i never forget everytime i saw you
Because i was happy that i met you
