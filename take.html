Take

Love is risk, love is chance,
A fearless leap, a fleeting dance.
It gives and takes, it wins, it errs,
Yet still, we chase—because it’s hers.

With every step, with every fall,
We rise again, to hear love's call.
It beckons us, to take the leap,
To trust the heart, to let love seep.

Through trials and tribulations, we roam,
In search of love, our hearts' sweet home.
We brave the storms, we weather the night,
To find the love, that shines with all its light.

And when we find it, oh, what a sight,
A love that's pure, a love that's bright.
It fills our hearts, it fills our soul,
A love that's worth, the risk, the chance, the goal.
