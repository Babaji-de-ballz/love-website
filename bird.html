Bird

Love is light, a soaring thing,
It gives the heart its chance to sing.
Yet if you clip its wings with doubt,
It cannot rise, it can’t break out.

But when you nurture it with gentle care,
And let it spread its wings to soar with flair,
It rises high, a radiant beam,
Illuminating all, a wondrous dream.

In this sweet freedom, love takes flight,
A majestic dance, a celestial sight.
With every beat, its beauty shines,
A love that's pure, a heart that's mine.
