Night

Love can fade when left alone,
A shadow cast, a heart of stone.
But even in the darkest sky,
A single star still dares to try.

Though darkness falls, and hope seems lost,
A spark remains, a love that's the cost.
For in the heart, a flame still glows,
A beacon bright, that guides us through the snows.

And when the night, its vigil keeps,
The star's soft light, our hearts it weeps.
For love, though faded, still remains,
A constant presence, that soothes our pains.
