Cruel

Love is sweet, but love is rough,
Sometimes warm, sometimes tough.
It gives, it takes, it lifts, it drowns,
A king’s gold or a beggar’s crown.

It's a mystery, a twisted game,
Where hearts are won, and hearts are tamed.
It's a risk we take, a chance we make,
To find the treasure, or the heartbreak's ache.

Yet still we seek, still we desire,
This love that's worth, our heart's on fire.
For in its depths, we find our own,
A reflection of the love we've known.
