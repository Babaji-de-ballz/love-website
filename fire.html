Fire

Love is heat, a roaring spark,
A light that glows within the dark.
But uncontrolled, it burns too fast,
Leaving only ash to last.

Like wildfire, it sweeps through the soul,
Leaving scars that never grow old.
Yet, tempered with care, it warms the heart,
A gentle flame that never departs.

In balance, it burns, a steady fire,
Warming the depths of love's desire.
Guided by wisdom, it shines so bright,
Illuminating love's pure delight.
