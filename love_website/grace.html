Grace

Love moves soft, with hands so kind,
A gentle touch, a heart that binds.
It does not force, it does not break,
But nurtures all for love’s own sake.

With whispered words, it soothes the soul,
A calming peace, that makes us whole.
It lifts the fallen, and heals the pain,
And brings hope to the heart, again and again.

In love's embrace, we find our nest,
A haven safe, where we can rest.
It surrounds us, with its warmth and light,
And fills our hearts, with pure delight.
