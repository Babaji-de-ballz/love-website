Break

In quiet moments, hearts align,
A spark ignites, two souls entwine.
From shadows deep, new light arrives,
In love's embrace, the spirit thrives.

A gentle touch can mend the past,
With open eyes, each bond is cast.
Dreams painted bright, once dimmed and gray,
In love's sweet breath, they find their way.

No longer lost, but found anew,
With every heartbeat, love shines through.
Transforming hearts with tender grace,
In love's pure light, we find our place.
