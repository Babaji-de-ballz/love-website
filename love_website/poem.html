Love's Daring Chase

By jed Luis Canosa

Love can be a problematic thing,
It can make us be loyal like a dog,
But sly like a fox,
Pushing us outside our box.

Love can teach us different lessons,
Love can change a person,
Though we shouldn't cross the line
Between love and obsession.

It can lift us high like a bird in flight,
Or trap us deep in endless night.
A force so kind yet cruel and wild,
That turns the wise into a child.

Love can heal, but love can break,
It’s a gamble, a risk we take.
A burning fire, warm and bright,
Yet blinding when it shines too white.

So tread with care, but love with grace,
For love is life’s most daring chase.
