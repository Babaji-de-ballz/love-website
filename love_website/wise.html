Wise

By kris ann Aquino

Love is not just a fleeting spark,
Not just a feeling that glows in the dark.
It's a lesson wrapped in joy and pain,
A story of sunshine, a dance in the rain.

At first, I thought love was all about smiles,
Butterflies, laughter, and endless miles.
But love whispered softly, "There's more to me,"
And slowly, I learned what love should be.

Love is not measured by words alone,
Not just in promises beautifully sewn.
It's in the silence when words run dry,
In the steady hand when you need to cry.

Love is patience when tempers ignite,
Choosing to stay when it's easier to fight.
It's in letting go when holding too tight
Means clipping the wings that long for flight.
