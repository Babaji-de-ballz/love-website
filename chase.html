Chase

We run, we fall, we rise, we fight,
We reach for love with all our might.
For though it flees, we still pursue—
For what is life if love is through?

Through every test, through every strife,
We'll chase the dream, of a loving life.
For in its warmth, we find our home,
A place to heal, to be made whole.

And when at last, we find our way,
To a love that stays, through every day,
We'll hold it close, we'll cherish every part,
And know that love, is the greatest art.
