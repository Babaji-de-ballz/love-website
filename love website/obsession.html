Obsession

A fire that burns too fierce, too bright,
Consumes the day, devours the night.
Love should lift, not tie or chain,
Or leave behind a trail of pain.

For love's true flame, should warm and guide,
Not scorch or burn, with a possessive pride.
It should set free, not hold in sway,
And bring joy to each new day.

In tender touch, and gentle care,
Love's flame should burn, with a love that's fair.
It should illuminate, not cast a shade,
And in its warmth, our hearts be made.
