Sly

Love can hide behind a smile,
A trickster’s charm, a fleeting guile.
It speaks in riddles, plays its game,
Yet leaves us never quite the same.

With whispered secrets, it entices the heart,
A siren's call, that sets us apart.
It weaves a web, of intrigue and desire,
And beckons us, to a love that's on fire.

In love's disguise, it slips away,
A shape-shifter, that changes each day.
It hides in plain sight, with a wink and a grin,
And leaves us searching, for the love within.

Yet even as it hides, it leaves a mark,
A memory that lingers, a heart that's left to spark.
For love's true nature, is a mystery so grand,
It transforms us, with a single, loving hand.
