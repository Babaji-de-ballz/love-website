Problematic

Love can be a twisted road,
A heavy weight, a shifting load.
A dream that turns to sleepless nights,
A whispered hope, a silent fight.

It winds and turns, through joy and pain,
A journey taken, with heart and brain.
It tests our will, our strength, our soul,
And pushes us, to our deepest goal.

Through every step, through every fall,
Love's lessons learned, stand tall.
For in its depths, we find our own,
A reflection of the love we've known.

And when the road, seems long and grey,
And love's weight, feels like it's here to stay,
We'll find the strength, to carry on,
And in love's darkness, a light will be born.
