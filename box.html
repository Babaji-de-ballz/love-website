Box

We build our walls, so strong, so tall,
Afraid to trip, afraid to fall.
Yet love will push, will pry, will break,
And free the heart for its own sake.

For love is brave, and love is kind,
It sees the beauty left behind.
It gently lifts the mask we wear,
And shows us who we truly are.

In this sweet freedom, we find our voice,
A whispered truth, a heartfelt choice.
To let love in, to let love stay,
And find the courage to live another way.
